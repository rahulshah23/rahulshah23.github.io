About Mirage Barbershop!

Are you looking for a fresh and original cut? Well look no further. Mirage Barbershop has been serving clients in the Ann Arbor Metro Detroit area for over 30 years and our customers are very satidified. Don't believe us? Well just check out our reviews page.

We offer a variety of different haircuts at several different price points. If you are a customer and you refer someone, you will get your next haircut 50% off! Our number 1 priority is to make sure our customers our happy so we would really love to hear your feedback.

Through this pandemic, we are taking several precautions that you should know about. We truly value our customers safety. Masks are required inside our store. All of our barbers will be wearing mask. Every hour, we sanitize each station. When a new customer walks in they sanitize their hands and their temperature will be taken. We want to make sure everyone can have a safe and enjoyable time!

Mirage Barbershop prides itself in 3 core values. Reliability, Satisfaction, and Authenticity. We want to make sure you look your absolute best!
