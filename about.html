About Mirage Barbershop!

Mirage Barbershop has been offering professional hairstyling services to the Ann Arbor, MI area since 1917. We are located on Packard street and look to expand our business into downtown Ann Arbor as well.

We provide hair care services that can be tailored to virtually any style. By providing scissor cuts, clipper cuts and styling we help our customers look fashionable at an affordable price.
